WELCOME TO TIGERLILY

PURPOSE

Tigerlily is a platform to enhance and track your dimensions of wellness. Please enjoy the to-do list, fitness, meal plan, budgeting, and journal features in this aesthetic workspace.

DIMENSIONS OF WELLNESS

Wellness involves eight interconnected dimensions: physical, intellectual, emotional, social, spiritual, occupational, financial, and environmental. It is known that neglect towards any of the dimensions can negatively impact the others, ultimately affecting your overall health, well-being, and quality of life. However, a perfect balance isn't suggested; rather an authentic "personal harmony" that aligns with your individual proiorities and values should be strived for.

PHYSICAL

  • Encompasses all aspects of your body's health including, nutrition, exercise, weight management, ergonomics, tobacco use, disease, disease prevention, and more

EMOTIONAL

  • Encompasses all aspects of wellbeing regarding your knowledge and skills to identify personal feelings, then handle those emotions properly

INTELLECTUAL

  • Encompasses all aspects of your brain health and growth achieved through engaging in though-provoking mental activities

SOCIAL

  • Encompasses all aspects of wellbeing related to your social connections, relationships, and personal expression

SPIRITUAL

  • Encompasses all aspects of wellbeing pertaining to your search for purpose and meaning in life

OCCUPATIONAL

  • Encompasses all aspects relating to your feelings of personal fulfillment and satisfation in your work life

ENVIRONMENTAL

  • Encompasses all aspects of the relationship between your environment and your health, including how your surroundings can impact your well-being

FINANCIAL

  • Encompasses all aspects of your financial health, encompassing your understanding and ability to plan your finances and manage your expenses effectively
